# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Individuals using Caledoro for personal focus, study sessions, and work sprints. They run a timer while checking daily quests and subtasks, often in calm, low-distraction environments.

## Product Purpose

Provide a lightweight, offline-first focus companion that blends a Pomodoro timer with a daily quest checklist so users can stay present, track progress, and finish meaningful work without stress.

## Brand Personality

Cozy, calm, tactile. The experience should feel warm, grounded, and gently encouraging rather than clinical or overly gamified.

## Anti-references

- Clinical SaaS dashboards with sharp lines and rigid grids
- Neon tech aesthetics or high-saturation cyber palettes
- Heavy, loud gradient treatments

## Design Principles

1. Prioritize calm focus over density or speed.
2. Use gentle, tactile cues to guide attention.
3. Keep interactions lightweight and reassuring.
4. Favor clarity and breathing room over visual noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG AA contrast targets. Respect user comfort with subtle motion and avoid overstimulation.
